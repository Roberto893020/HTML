What is our goal

Our goal here at the Fate figure store is to spread our love for the Fate series. We have spent more than 10 years reading visual novels , Watching Fate anime, and collecting figures. As we approach our final days, we do not want our figures to go to waste so we are selling every fate figure we have ever collected. This means that we have more than 1 million figures available to sell, so dont be shy. All we ask is that you treat the figures with love and that you continue to keep the Fate series alive.
